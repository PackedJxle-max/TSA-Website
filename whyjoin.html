Why You Should Join

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization that empowers students to explore STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) through hands-on competitions, leadership opportunities, and real-world problem solving. By joining TSA, students develop critical thinking, collaboration, and technical skills that prepare them for future careers. It also offers a chance to connect with like-minded peers and showcase innovations at regional, state, and national conferences. TSA is a fun and inspiring way to turn your passion for technology into real achievement.

Competitions

One of the most exciting reasons to join the Technology Student Association (TSA) is the wide variety of competitions that let you explore and showcase your skills in STEM and leadership. Whether you're passionate about coding, engineering, fashion design, architecture, video production, or even public speaking, TSA has over 70 competitive events tailored to different interests and abilities. These events challenge you to solve real-world problems, often in teams, and give you the chance to compete at the regional, state, and even national level. Not only do TSA competitions build your resume and college applications, but they also help you grow as a problem-solver, collaborator, and leader—all while having fun and making friends with students across the country.
